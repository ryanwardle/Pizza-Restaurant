About Godfather's Pizza

Godfather's Pizza was founded in 1973, when Anthony Capizzi moved from Brooklyn, NY to Columbus, OH. Upset that pizza in the midwest was not what he called "real pizza", so he decided to solve the problem himself. He built a small pizza shop that he ran by himself, soon business picked up and he was in need of a staff, 45 years later Godfather's pizza is still serving the only "real pizza" in Columbus.
